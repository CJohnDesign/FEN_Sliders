## How the Plan Works

<v-clicks>

1. **Enroll through BWA membership**.
2. **Access healthcare services** with reduced costs.
3. **Submit bills to MBR for repricing**.
4. **Receive Explanation of Benefits (EOB)**.
5. **Pay final bill based on negotiated rates**.

</v-clicks>
