## How International Benefits Administrators Works

<v-clicks>

1. Enroll through **NCE**
2. Submit claim forms
3. Processing by IBA
4. Receive **Explanation of Benefits (EOB)**
5. Resolve any discrepancies
6. **Benefit Payments**

</v-clicks>
