## Plan Overview

The "Advanced Wellness Plus" insurance plan is a Limited Benefit Health Insurance Plan underwritten by American Financial Security Life Insurance Co. It is designed to provide essential health and wellness benefits to its members, particularly focusing on small and medium-sized businesses through its association with the National Congress of Employers. This plan aims to supplement major medical plans by offering capped benefits for various healthcare services.

## Core Plan Elements

- **Key Features and Benefits:**
  - medical benefits across multiple tiers.
  - Coverage for hospital confinement, office visits, emergency room services, mental health, and more.
  - Additional benefits like accidental death and critical illness coverage.

- **Coverage Details:**
  - Benefits vary significantly across the different plan tiers, with higher tiers offering greater coverage amounts.
  - A 30-day waiting period for sickness benefits and a 12-month exclusion for pre-existing conditions apply.

- **Eligibility Requirements:**
  - Available to adult members aged 18-65 and dependent children aged 1-25.

## Common Service Features

- **Network Access:**
  - Access to the Aetna Dental Access Network for dental care discounts.
  - Prescription discount program and OUTLOOK Vision Network for optical savings.

- **Claims Process:**
  - Easy claim submission through mail or electronic means.
  - Support for medical bill negotiations.

- **Support Services:**
  - 24/7 health information line and nurse help line to assist members.
  - Savings on alternative medicine, chiropractic care, and hearing programs.

## Limitations and Definitions

- **Important Exclusions:**
  - No coverage for treatments not medically necessary or prescribed.
  - Excludes dental procedures unrelated to accidental injury, cosmetic surgeries, and experimental treatments.
  - Excludes injuries related to war, self-inflicted harm, or illegal activities.
  - 30-day waiting period for sickness benefits and a 12-month exclusion for pre-existing conditions.

- **Key Terms Defined:**
  - **Hospital Confinement:** Coverage for each day a plan member is confined to a hospital.
  - **Primary Care Visit:** Benefits for visits to a primary healthcare provider.
  - **Specialty Care Visit:** Coverage for consultations with specialist providers.
  - **Emergency Room:** Benefits for emergency room visits due to acute medical conditions.
