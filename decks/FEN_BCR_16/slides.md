## How Medical Bill Repricer Works

<v-clicks>

1. Enroll through **FirstEnroll**
2. Choose any doctor
3. Prepay $25-$50
4. Receive **EOB** (Explanation of Benefits)
5. Providers paid 150%-200% Medicare
6. **Negotiate Costs**
