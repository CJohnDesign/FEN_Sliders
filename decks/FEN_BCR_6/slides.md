## How Reference-Based Pricing Works

<v-clicks>

1. Enroll through **American Public Life**
2. Select any doctor or specialist
3. Pay pre-set fees
4. Receive Explanation of Benefits **(EOB)**
5. Reduce remaining balance
6. **Cost Savings Achieved**
