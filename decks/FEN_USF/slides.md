## Definitions and Limitations

<v-click>

**Exclusions**
- Pre-existing conditions not covered for 12 months
- TMJ disorders, elective surgeries
- Routine vision care, blood treatments
</v-click>

<v-click>

**Limitations**
- 30-day waiting period for sickness
- Travel-related exclusions
</v-click>

<v-click>

**Specific Exclusions**
- Accidents without a valid license
- Non-passenger aircraft travel
</v-click>

<v-click>

**Additional Exclusions**
- Rest cures, custodial care
- Prescription drugs unless specified
</v-click>
